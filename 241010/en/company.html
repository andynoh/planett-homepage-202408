About us
History

Your gateway to seamless
Global Connectivity

In 2004, Planet T launched Korea's first portal roaming service, addressing communication challenges for travelers abroad.
With a range of offerings, including SIM cards, eSIMs, and pocket WiFi, Planet T ensures a convenient and reliable communication experience no matter where you are in the world.

We have been growing by providing customers with the best solutions through continuous technological development and global network expansion.

Additionally, backed by a strong financial foundation and transparent corporate governance, as demonstrated by our excellent credit ratings (A or above), we are committed to creating customer-centric value and achieving sustainable growth.
By securing a competitive edge in the market, we are positioning ourselves as a trusted partner among our stakeholders.

Planet T satisfies the needs
of various customers.

  • Individual Users

    We offer SIM cards, eSIMs, and portable Wi-Fi to overseas travelers and business professionals, ensuring seamless data communication and convenient connectivity.

  • Corporate Customers

    Our B2B eSIM purchasing platform delivers efficient business solutions for companies that require bulk service usage.

  • Telecom Operators
    and Partners

    We collaborate with various telecom operators and partners to provide our services, continually evolving through these partnerships.

Global network

Planet T is a global company with subsidiaries in Hong Kong, the United States, and other countries.
Each subsidiary has a deep understanding of local market characteristics and customer needs,
allowing us to offer tailored services that create a distinct competitive advantage in the global market.
